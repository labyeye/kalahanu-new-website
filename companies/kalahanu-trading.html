Who We Are

About the Company

Kalahanu Bothra Trading Pvt Ltd is the authorized Bajaj three-wheeler distributor under the Kalahanu Bothra Group umbrella. Headquartered in Patna, Bihar, we specialize in delivering durable cargo and passenger three-wheelers designed for urban last-mile connectivity and rural freight mobility.

With a robust distribution network spanning Bihar, we provide end-to-end solutions — from vehicle sales and financing assistance to after-sales service — ensuring every business owner gets maximum value from their investment.

  • Authorized Bajaj three-wheeler distributor for Bihar
  • Comprehensive cargo & passenger vehicle portfolio
  • Financing & documentation assistance
  • Dedicated after-sales and service support
