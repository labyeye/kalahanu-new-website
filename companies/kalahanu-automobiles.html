Who We Are

About the Company

Kalahanu Automobile Pvt Ltd is the flagship commercial vehicle arm of the Kalahanu Bothra Group, headquartered in Patna, Bihar. As an authorized Tata Motors partner, we deliver reliable Light Commercial Vehicles (LCV), Intermediate Commercial Vehicles (ICV), and Heavy Commercial Vehicles (HCV) to businesses across Bihar.

With over 15 years of operations, we have built end-to-end infrastructure — from modern showrooms and test drive facilities to a full-service workshop and genuine spare parts supply — ensuring every customer enjoys maximum uptime and operational efficiency.

  • Full range: LCV, ICV, HCV trucks and buses
