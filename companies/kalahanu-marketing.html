Who We Are

About the Company

Kalahanu Marketing Pvt Ltd is the marketing and distribution arm of the Kalahanu Bothra Group. We specialize in the authorized distribution of genuine Tata spare parts across Bihar, combined with strategic brand marketing initiatives that help automotive and consumer brands expand their reach and visibility.

With a network covering all 38 districts of Bihar and deep relationships with retailers, workshops, and fleet operators, we are the preferred distribution partner for brands seeking genuine market penetration in Eastern India's fastest-growing automotive market.

  • Authorized Tata genuine spare parts distributor for Bihar
  • Pan-Bihar distribution network across all 38 districts
  • Strategic brand partnerships and marketing campaigns
  • Customer-centric solutions with data-driven marketing
