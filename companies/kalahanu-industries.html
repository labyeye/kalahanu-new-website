Who We Are

About the Company

Kalahanu Bothra Industries Pvt Ltd operates Bihar's state-of-the-art Authorized Testing Station (ATS) and registered vehicle scrapping facility. As part of the Kalahanu Bothra Group, we ensure road safety, regulatory compliance, and environmental responsibility for all vehicle categories.

Our ATS is equipped with the latest computerized testing equipment to perform fitness certifications, emission tests, brake tests, and structural safety checks as mandated by the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways. Our registered scrapping facility ensures end-of-life vehicles are processed in a safe, documented, and eco-friendly manner.

  • Government-approved Authorized Testing Station (ATS)
  • Computerized fitness certification for all vehicle types
  • Registered vehicle scrapping under MoRTH guidelines
  • Scrapping certificate enabling new vehicle purchase benefits

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about vehicle testing and scrapping in Bihar

All commercial vehicles (trucks, buses, taxis, autos) require a mandatory Fitness Certificate under the Motor Vehicles Act. Private vehicles older than 15 years also need periodic fitness testing as per new regulations.

Our computerized testing process takes approximately 1.5 to 2 hours per vehicle. With prior appointment, we guarantee same-day certificate issuance for vehicles that meet all fitness criteria.

You need: original RC (Registration Certificate), valid ID proof, vehicle insurance (if applicable), and a NOC if the vehicle has a loan. Our team assists with all documentation at the facility.

You receive a Deposit of Vehicle certificate which entitles you to: waiver of road tax on a new vehicle purchase, discount on new vehicle registration fees, and additional incentives from select manufacturers including Tata Motors and Bajaj.

Walk-ins are accepted but appointments are recommended to avoid waiting. Book online via our contact form or call us to schedule your preferred time slot. Fleet operators can book bulk slots.
