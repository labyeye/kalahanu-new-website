Who We Are

About the Company

Kalahanu Bothra Agencies Pvt Ltd is the authorized Bajaj two-wheeler dealership under the Kalahanu Bothra Group. Based in Patna, Bihar, we offer the widest selection of Bajaj motorcycles and scooters — from everyday commuters to performance sports bikes and electric vehicles.

Our state-of-the-art showroom features dedicated Pulsar and Dominar experience zones, trained riding advisors, and an in-house service center with certified technicians — ensuring every customer gets the perfect ride backed by world-class support.

  • Authorized Bajaj two-wheeler dealership for Bihar
  • Full range: commuter, sports, premium, and EV segments
  • Dedicated Pulsar and Dominar experience zones
  • In-house service center with certified technicians
