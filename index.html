Who We Are

About the Group

Kalahanu Bothra Group is a diversified business conglomerate headquartered in Patna, Bihar. Founded with a vision of bringing quality commercial solutions to the people of Bihar, the group has grown to become one of the most trusted names in the region.

Over 15 years, we have built an ecosystem that touches every aspect of commercial mobility — from vehicle sales and financing to industrial testing and wide-reach distribution across the state.

  • Authorized multi-brand dealer and distributor
  • Strong distribution network spanning all of Bihar
  • End-to-end commercial vehicle solutions
  • Decades of customer trust and satisfaction
