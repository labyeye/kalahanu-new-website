About Kalahanu Both Group

A legacy of trust, excellence, and automotive leadership in Bihar.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Kalahanu Both Group has grown from a single dealership into one of Bihar's most diversified and respected business conglomerates. With a primary focus on the automotive sector, we have established ourselves as the go-to partner for global brands seeking a strong presence in the region.

Over the last 15 years, we have served thousands of customers across Bihar, providing them with high-quality commercial vehicles, passenger two-wheelers, and exceptional after-sales support. Our commitment to integrity and customer satisfaction has earned us multiple awards and a loyal customer base.

Our Mission

To provide world-class automotive solutions and distribution services that empower the businesses and individuals of Bihar, while maintaining the highest standards of transparency and service.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred and trusted automotive partner in Eastern India, known for our innovative approach, skilled workforce, and commitment to regional development.

Our Journey
