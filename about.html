About Kalahanu Both Group

A legacy of trust, excellence, and automotive leadership in Bihar.

Our Evolution

Way back in 1980s, the auspicious hands of late Shri Hanuman Mal Bothra laid the foundation stone of the KalaHanu group with organisations diversified into various leading sectors such as FMCG, media, telecom, commodity trading, automobiles and financial services. Since inception, KalaHanu has witnessed growth in phenomenal numbers not only in terms of further diversification, turnover and profitability, but also in garnering the trust and support of ever spawning employees and stakeholders. Today, grossing over more than Rs.1500 crore, the group has established a formidable position through consistent efforts and keen dedication, partnering its success with strong committed teams and stakeholders who are ever fervent and passionately infusing KalaHanu with vitality and energy, the quintessential ingredients for business growth.
